nimio.us

Extravagant packages for the Nim programming language

Support

Please submit pull requests on GitHub or email us at info@nimio.us if any of our packages are out of date or contain bugs. All other related problems are best handled through nimious tagged StackOverflow questions. You may also find us in the official Freenode IRC channel for the Nim programming language community.

About

nimio.us was founded by Gerke Max Preussner in December 2014 with the goal to promote and spread the Nim programming language. Nim is a modern, statically typed, imperative and cross-platform systems programming language that gives programmers ultimate power without compromises on runtime efficiency.
