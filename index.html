Support

Please submit pull requests or issues on GitHub, or email us at info@nimio.us if any of our packages are out of date or can be improved. All other issues are best handled via nimious tagged questions on StackOverflow. You may also find us in #nim, the official community chat channel on Freenode IRC.

If you are a hardware vendor and would like your products to be integrated with Nim, the fastest way is to provide us with your SDK and, if possible, a development kit for testing. If you know of a cool product that should be integrated, please contact us!

About

nimio.us was founded by Gerke Max Preussner in December 2014 with the goal to promote and spread the Nim programming language. Nim is a modern, statically typed, imperative and cross-platform systems programming language that gives programmers ultimate power without compromises on runtime efficiency.
